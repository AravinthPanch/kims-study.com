About KIMS

Leading educationists of medical science with extensive experiences have come together to train the students who have trained abroad and come back to India to clear the medical screening tests in our country to become doctors. These trainers make use of their expertise and deep knowledge of the market to train these aspirants to fulfill their dreams and become professionals. The students get close guidance and are enabled to streamline their studies to crack the examinations under strict supervision. It makes a lot of sense for the budding doctors to opt for Karol Institute of Medical Science to reach their goal without groping in the dark.

KIMS is backed by the Karol Educational Foundation, which is in turn run by doctors who had received their medical education abroad and passed out the screening test with flying colours, and are now thriving practitioners themselves.
